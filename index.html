Hi my name is Krystal Tran and I am 18 years old. I am currently a first year student at the University of California, Riverside pursuing in Sociology. I am a young adult with many experiences and interests. My current interests include binge watching youtube videos or listening to my favorite artists on repeat to relieve some stress. I currently have been pursuing in editing personal websites as well as some journaling to express my own creativity. As a young woman who has just started college, I do have a dream career I hope to pursue after I finish school. My current dream career is to become a social worker where I am able to help foster children stay on a successful path to pursue a beautiful future for themselves. I grew up not having as much as those around me, but my parents were an impactful role model that kept pushing me to try harder and to chase after a dream. That is what I hope to be for these foster children in the future one day.

Although I have not had much experience in this type of social work, I am currently working towards it. I have had jobs in the past to help me out financially before I left home for college that included foods service and sales associate positions. I have worked on my communication with the customers I have interacted with and made sure they always left with a smile on their face. I have learned how to manage my time as well as how to greet others in a way where they are able to feel special. Discipline, responsibility, and communication are big factors I will take into the future with me to gain more experience before I take a step into pursuing my dream career. Through these part-time jobs, I have gained experience that I will bring along with me in the future to help me communicate with the children I will be working with. I will do my best to make sure the children I work with in the future will grow up with a smile on their face.

Foster kids are known to struggle a lot in school as well as in their own personal lives. I want them to be reminded everyday through my work that they are someone who is important for this world and that all the work that they will do will matter for the future. I hope in the future I am able to travel all around the world to share my knowledge with the children that might have a mindset where they think they will not be able to make it. I want to be an impactful role model as well as someone they can rely on to help them know that they are not alone. This is a dream I hope to pursue in the future. Since I know how much of an influence I can be in someone's life, I am willing to do whatever it takes to make that change in the world. I will continue to work hard towards this goal as a sociology student at UCR.
